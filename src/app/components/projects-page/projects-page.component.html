Running a small farm takes constant upkeep - everything from feeding the animals, cleaning stalls and maintaining our pastures. But we tend to go beyond general upkeep, as our farm was in need of many major improvements!

Over the years, thousands of feet of new fencing has been installed to keep our animals in, as well cross fencing to support our rotational grazing program. Our barn got a new roof and new stain (click on the Farm Projects page to learn about this traditional stain from Sweden). The barn is now providing a warm and dry environment for lambing. More recently, a new barn addition will add 750 square feet of lambing space and storage. Click on the sections below follow our journey of converting our once neglected farm into a productive sheep and produce operation, not to mention that our farm is now downright beautiful!
