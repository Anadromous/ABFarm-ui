As with many initiatives on our farm, our plans to open a camping area is still in the works. Our hope is to be able to invite guests to stay overnight on our farm in a wooded area (that was once covered in blackberries!). We would like to offer our guests a retreat from city life, or a stopover for bikers, or for anyone looking for a quick getaway to the country. We are close to the Banks-Vernonia trail for hiking or biking.
Guests are also welcome to take part in farm activities including feeding the animals or any of our farm chores.
